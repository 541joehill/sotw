Meet Your Guide - Wil

Wil has been fishing the rivers of Western Oregon his whole life. What started as a childhood obsession quickly grew into a lifelong passion—and now, a full-time career for over 10 years. As a dedicated guide, Wil offers unforgettable fishing experiences targeting some of the region’s most prized species, including chinook salmon, steelhead, and more.


Whether you're a seasoned angler chasing your next trophy fish or a first-timer just looking to enjoy a relaxing day on the water, Wil tailors each trip to fit your goals. Bring the whole family or come solo—either way, you’ll be in good hands with a guide who knows the water, the fish, and how to have a great time.
